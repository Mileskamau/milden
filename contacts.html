Frequently Asked Questions

What types of tours does Milden Adventure offer?

We offer a wide range of adventure tours including hiking expeditions, wildlife safaris, mountain climbing, cultural immersion experiences, and water-based adventures. Our tours are categorized by difficulty level and duration to suit different preferences.

How do I book a tour with Milden Adventure?

You can book directly through our website, by calling our office, or by visiting us in person. For bookings, we require a 30% deposit to secure your spot, with the balance due 30 days before departure.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Cancellations made more than 60 days before departure receive a full refund. Between 30-60 days, we refund 50% of your payment. Cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable, but we offer trip credits for future bookings.

What should I pack for an adventure tour?

We provide a detailed packing list specific to each tour upon booking. Generally, we recommend comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, a reusable water bottle, personal medications, sunscreen, and any necessary travel documents. We provide specialized equipment for specific activities.

Do you offer custom or private tours?

Yes! We specialize in creating custom itineraries for individuals, families, and groups. Contact our adventure specialists to discuss your interests, group size, and preferred dates. We'll design a unique experience tailored to your preferences.
